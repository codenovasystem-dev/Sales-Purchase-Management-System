# SalesIQ - Executive Summary

**Business-ready analytics platform for e-commerce companies**

---

## 📊 What is SalesIQ?

SalesIQ is a **real-time business intelligence platform** that helps e-commerce companies:
- 📈 Monitor sales in real-time
- 📦 Manage inventory efficiently
- 🔮 Forecast revenue accurately
- 👥 Control user access securely

Think of it as a **dashboard that lets business owners see their entire operation at a glance**.

---

## 🎯 The Problem We Solve

### Before SalesIQ
```
❌ Data scattered across platforms
❌ Inventory surprises (stockouts/overstock)
❌ Can't see sales trends quickly
❌ Manual reporting takes hours
❌ No revenue visibility
❌ Hard to give team access
```

### After SalesIQ
```
✅ Everything in one place
```
